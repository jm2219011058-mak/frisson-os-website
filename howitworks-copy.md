### 01 — Global Residence Networks · 全球寓所网络
**Title (EN):** One Key, Every Horizon
**标题 (ZH):** 一把钥匙,通往每一处地平线

**Body (EN):**
To own with Frisson is to own access to a living network of residences set in the world's most extraordinary places — from a Sky Atelier rising over the Gulf capitals to the Collection Residences of old Europe. Reserve nights at any property in the network, months in advance; where Frisson stands, you are home.

**正文 (ZH):**
与 Frisson 同行的"拥有",是拥有一张遍布世界非凡之地的活体寓所网络——从矗立于海湾都会的 Sky Atelier,到老欧洲的 Collection Residences。可提前数月,在网络中任一处预订入住;Frisson 立于何处,家便在何处。

---

### 02 — Fractional Property Ownership · 分权共有产权
**Title (EN):** Own a Fraction, Gain the Whole
**标题 (ZH):** 拥有一份,尽享其全

**Body (EN):**
Each residence is divided into shares. Hold the fraction that fits your life and gain the full grade of a Frisson home — its architecture, its service, its asset value — without the burden of sole ownership. A share is a lasting, transferable asset: release it in a secondary market whenever you choose.

**正文 (ZH):**
每一处寓所被划分为若干份额。持有契合你生活的那一份,即可尽享 Frisson 寓所的完整规格——建筑、服务与资产价值——而无须独自承担全权持有之重。份额是可传承、可转让的长期资产:你亦可在二级市场,按己意随时释出。

---

### 03 — Full Living-Health Asset Hospitality · 全周期人居健康资产（Bio-AI OS）
**Title (EN):** A Home That Remembers Your Body
**标题 (ZH):** 一处记得你身体的居所

**Body (EN):**
This is where Frisson reaches beyond property. Through **Sona**, our Bio-AI OS, every residence quietly accumulates a long-term record of how you live and heal — how you sleep, breathe, and recover — as a private asset that belongs to you alone. **You hold its private key.** Arrive at any Frisson residence in the world, and that key restores your personalized environmental-intelligence model: light, sound, temperature, scent, and air, tuned to your body from the very first moment. Your health data never belongs to a place — it travels with you, and every place remembers you.

**正文 (ZH):**
这是 Frisson 超越"地产"的地方。透过 **Sona**——我们的 Bio-AI OS,每一处寓所都在静默中,累积一份关乎你如何栖居、如何自愈的长期档案——你如何入眠、呼吸、恢复——成为只属于你一人的私有资产。**这份数据的私钥,由你持有。** 抵达世界任一处 Frisson 寓所,这把私钥便会重建你的个性化环境智能模型:光、声、温度、香氛与空气,自第一刻起,便依你的身体而调谐。你的健康数据从不属于某个地方——它随你而行,而每一处,都记得你。
