Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FRISSON ("FRISSON", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you visit frisson-os.com (the "Site") or submit a form to us. We are committed to handling your information with care and only collecting what we need.

1. Information we collect

We collect the information you choose to provide through our forms, which may include: your name, email address, country of residence, phone number, the role or category you select, and any message or details you write. If you tick a consent box, we also record that you agreed to receive communications from us.

When you visit the Site, our hosting provider may automatically collect limited technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages viewed, for security and basic analytics.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to: (a) review and respond to your application or inquiry; (b) evaluate participation in the Advocater Program or related initiatives; (c) contact you about your submission; (d) send you communications where you have consented; and (e) operate, secure, and improve the Site.

3. Legal bases

Where applicable law (such as the GDPR) requires a legal basis, we rely on: your consent (which you may withdraw at any time); our legitimate interest in reviewing applications and operating the Site; and, where relevant, steps taken at your request before entering into any agreement.

4. How your information is handled and shared

Form submissions are processed and stored by our website host and form provider (Netlify) on our behalf. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the Site, and where required by law. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Cookies and analytics

The Site aims to use minimal cookies. Some cookies may be set by our hosting provider for security and performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect some functionality.

6. Data retention

We keep your information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described above, to comply with legal obligations, or to resolve disputes. When it is no longer needed, we delete or anonymise it.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information, to object to certain processing, to data portability, and to withdraw consent. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at mak@frisson-os.com.

8. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including where our service providers operate. Where required, we take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

9. Children

The Site is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will reflect the most recent revision.

11. Contact

For any question about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, contact mak@frisson-os.com.
